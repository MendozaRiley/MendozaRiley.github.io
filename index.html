About the Artist

Growing up, I always used to feel a little detached from reality. It might have just been my overactive imagination, but regardless I would often let my fanciful ideas dictate my thoughts and actions. In fact, I felt more grounded in fictional worlds than the one I lived in. I was always fascinated by the new realities created within novels, stories, video games, tv shows, and movies because of the depth of the characters, their relationships, and their backgrounds.
