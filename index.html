About the Artist

As a digital media artist, I try to experiment with numerous different media from digital illustration to video art to web design. My artistic practice also includes the use of other mediums such as pencil, ink, charcoal, and paint. Currently, I am pursuing new ways to incorporate interactivity into my artwork in order to deepen the connection between the viewer and the work. Read More.
