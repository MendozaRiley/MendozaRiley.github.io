"Love Will Keep Us Alive," 2022

"Love Will Keep Us Alive" is a short collection of love poetry written with the assistance of two AI text generation tools, including LyricStudio by WaveAI. I also designed this poetry book using cultivated AI generated art. This poetry book is organized by the eight ancient Greek types of love: Agape, Storge, Philia, Ludus, Eros, Mania, Pragma, and Philautia. Each section features 1-4 poems representative of its respective love type.
