"Immortal Glitch"




"Immortal Glitch," 2020

With an image from one of my favorite movies called "What We Do in the Shadows," I used Audacity to create a glitched version. The final glitched image shows the effects of numerous different changes and saves. I chose to glitch this image because I wanted to portray how the variations of vampires within movies and fiction creates a blurred idea of the supernatural vampire identity.
