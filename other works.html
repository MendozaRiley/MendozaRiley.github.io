Other Works

This collection of work includes a small variety of mediums from pencil to acrylic paint. Click on the title to view.


"Don't Look"

"Don't Look," Acrylic Paint on Canvas, 9" x 12",2019.

This painting represents contradiction. While the text commands the viewer not to look, the image draws them in, leading to the question of whether they should continue to look or not.


"Troll"


"Troll," 9" x 12", 2020.

I transferred a group of images onto a sheet of acrylic glass using acrylic matte medium to create a collage that represented the different layers of Twitter's virtual environment as an online discussion platform.


"Where Did the Time Go?"


"Where Did the Time Go?" Acrylic Paint on Canvas, 12" x 25", 2019.

This is an acrylic painting that deals with the relationship between time and humanity.

"Poetic Beauty," 2019.

"Wake Up"


"Wake Up," 2019.

"Unkillable Teacher"







"Unkillable Teacher," 2020.

This a wireframe model of Korosensei, a character from "Assassination Classroom." This piece deals with cross-sections and their relationship to form.


"Fairy House"


"Fairy House," 2020.

This is an experimention with materials in regards to texture, adhesion, and support. The piece is made up of CD cases, pipe cleaners, and moss that are arranged in order to transform the materials.


"Window"


"Window," 2020.

This is an experimention with materials in regards to texture, adhesion, and support. The piece is made up of acrylic glass, matte medium, and paint.
