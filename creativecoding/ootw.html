"Out of this World," p5.js, 2021

My initial approach to this project was exploratory. I was testing out certain functions and playing around with pieces of code from past sketches when I came up with the extraterrestrial theme for this drawing tool. At first, I was planning on creating a tool that would allow the user to sketch an extraterrestrial sighting scene. However, as I began creating my own sketches, I strayed away from the more representational drawing plan and began using the drawing tools objectively. While the subject matter of my tool is space themed, the tool seems to work well with abstract art styles involving a cool color scheme and a variety of geometric and organic shapes.

Drawing Tool Keys:

  • Q : This is the brush for the black and white lines that represent tree/grass like shapes of the alien sighting scene.
  • S : This is the brush for the random small white circles scattered around the sketches.
  • A : This is the brush for the alien figure that radiates light blue and purple circles from its core.
  • 1 : This is the brush for the first UFO shape that "comes in piece."
  • 2 : This is the brush for the second UFO shape with the white light emanating from the bottom.
  • 3 : This is the brush for the third UFO shape that emits the blue-ish green rays that will "destroy us all."
  • E : This is the brush for the color changing parallelograms.
  • B : Layers a light purple over the canvas.
  • P : Layers a dark purple over the canvas.
  • Space bar: Places the white moon is the center of the canvas.
