"Space and Time," p5.js, 2021

For this project, I wanted to continue with the space theme from past projects. I decided to portray the passage of time through a concise, and fictional version of the life of a star. This project acts as a clock, where the shifting size and color of the center star reveal how far into an hour the time is. As a result, the star explodes 24 times a day, respawning at the beginning of each hour. The first 12 hours of the day involve a warm color scheme, while the last 12 hours include a cool color scheme.
