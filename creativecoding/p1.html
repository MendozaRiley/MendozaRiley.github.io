Sketch 1, Java, 2021

This is the first sketch that I have ever made using Processing. I experimented with a multitude of different functions, commands, and control statements such as the println() command, the random command, and for loop statements. This sketch presents a cluster of shapes and colors that represent my attempts at understanding the behaviors of different code.
