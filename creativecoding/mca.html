"An Mc Adventure," p5.js, 2021

This is the first part of a story-based adventure game titled "An MC Adventure." Part I features two embedded mini-games that impact the player's ending in Part II. The game offers an outrageous storyline with characters based on celebrities and famous fictional characters in order to parody the nature of fanfiction and self-insert stories. This interface aims to comment on the idea of gamification by going over the top with the threatrics from vibrant colors to unnecessary sound effects to numerous interactive sprites. Additionally, considering how fanfiction and self-insert games are meant to simulate an alternate reality, I used more skeuomorphic design to highlight the reliance of this digital world on the people and events of the physical one. The interface includes a multitude of bitmaps and sound effects, an ungodly amount of different states, and multiple help screens for each main state. While the mini-games involve mouse driven navigation, the narratives scenes mainly use keyboard input. The three libraries I used are p5.sound, p5.scenemanager, and p5.play.
