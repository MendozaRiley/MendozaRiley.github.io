"An Mc Adventure," p5.js, 2021

This project is an interactive story game that follows a character named MC on his journey in search of his estranged twin sister named Y/N. MC must gain allies to face up against his evil manager, Mike Al Sara, on his search for his sister. This game is meant to playfully mimic fanfiction and self insert stories that revolve around well-known characters or celebrities which often involve entertainingly outrageous plot lines. The amount of allies that MC gains throughout the game affects which story ending the player receives. The game can be played through multiple times to achieve all of the possible story endings. I coded the game, and Lily Mendoza created the artwork. The game uses the following libraries: p5.sound, p5.scenemanager, and p5.play.
