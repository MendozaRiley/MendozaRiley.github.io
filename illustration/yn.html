"Not a Y/N Fantasy," Interactive Story, 2020

This is an interactive story created with HTML, CSS, and Bootstrap 4 that allows the reader to step into the role of the main character of a story. Many aspects of the story are based on y/n fanfiction tropes in order to poke a bit of lighthearted fun at the genre that often romanticizes certain famous characters and individuals. The interactivity of the story helps the reader engage more with the work and allows them to dive even deeper into the main concept of idealization and how people or situations may not always turn out the way one was hoping.
