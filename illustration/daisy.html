"Daisy's Night Terrors," Video Game Art, 2022

This is a collection of illustrations that I created for a video game called "Daisy's Night Terrors." In this game, the player must help Daisy wake up from her nightmare. By avoiding the dream monsters and collecting flower petals, the player is able to help Daisy remember who she is so that she can escape from her dream realm.
