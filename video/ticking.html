"Ticking," Digital Video, 6 m 14 s, 2020

This video explores the interrelationship between light and darkness in people regarding how they deal with emotions of annoyance, anger, and aggravation. The narrative presents the seemingly comical interactions between two siblings who do not get along very well, as their disdain for each other grows. Instead of communicating their issues with one another, they bottle up their anger until they explode. The video concludes ambiguously, leaving the viewer alone with their own interpretation. This piece contains some violence.
Featuring: Gabe Mendoza and Connie Mendoza
