Personal Stamp, Woodcut of 3D Model, 2021

For my stamp, I wanted to create an image that represented me in some way. This design is a simplified version of the first painting that I sold when I was in high school. The shrunken prints, in particular, hold a specific significance. These prints are smaller than the rest and are on a clear material which represent how I am such a small part of the vast universe that we exist in. The purple color of the print symbolizes my identity (because I am purple). Thus, the shrunken prints reflect that although I may sometimes feel small, or invisible, I still have a soul; I'm still a person.
